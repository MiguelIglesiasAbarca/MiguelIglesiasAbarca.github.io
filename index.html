MY WORK & EXPERIENCE

Blue Demon

Inspired by a concept from Lost Bear Studios, I crafted this Blue Demon model from start to finish. The process included sculpting, retopology, UV mapping, baking, and texturing.

Nordic Axe

This stylized Nordic Axe, based on a concept art, showcases my skills in the entire workflow: sculpting, retopology, UV mapping, baking, and texturing. I experimented with ZBrush tools to achieve detailed craftsmanship and realistic fabric textures.

Scrap Gun

This Scrap Gun, used by RoadHog in Overwatch 2, was created using various images and angles due to the challenge of finding high-quality reference pictures. I worked on it from scratch, including blocking, modeling, sculpting, texturing, UV mapping, and baking.

Cuphead Liquid Simulations

A simple project used for testing liquid simulations, both realistic and stylized. The model was entirely created in Blender.

Magic Frog

This project is based on an intriguing concept art. The entire process, including texturing, was completed in ZBrush.

About Me

Hello! My name is Miguel, and I'm a passionate Videogame Developer and Designer at CITM | UPC. I have experience working with Blender, Maya, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Photoshop, and much more.

I also work with C++ and Unity, and I particularly enjoy Game and Level Design.

I believe that the combination of technical and creative skills is crucial in the world of game development. With an adaptable profile and a team-oriented mindset, I love contributing to collaborative environments while leveraging my technical expertise.

As a positive and motivated individual, I continuously seek opportunities for growth and learning within the landscape of game development. If you're interested in connecting, please feel free to reach out to me. I look forward to hear from you!
