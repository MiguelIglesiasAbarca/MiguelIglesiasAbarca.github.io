    SPITS & CRYPTS

    Spits and Crypts is a 2D RPG puzzle platformer, 2D engine-powered set in ancient Egypt. The gameplay focuses on exploration, allowing players to traverse Egyptian landscapes while uncovering secrets through puzzles and challenges. Additionally, the game features turn-based strategic combat with a variety of enemies and creatures, adding a unique dynamism to the setting.

    MIND'S ABYSS

    Minds Abyss is an unforgettable 3D cooperative adventure that explores the complexities of a retired miner’s mind as he battles Alzheimer’s. Players take on the roles of a dream and a nightmare, traveling through the miner’s mental islands while being pursued by the ominous specter of oblivion.

    ABOUT ME

    Hi! I’m a passionate Game Developer and Designer currently studying at CITM | UPC. Ever since I got my hands on my first tablet as a kid, I’ve been fascinated by video games and how they’re made. I started by creating simple 2D platformers with basic tools, making my friends play them just to see their reactions.

    As I grew older, my passion only deepened. I explored level design through in-game editors in Fortnite and Roblox, pushing the limits of what I could create.

    The real turning point came when I realized I could turn this passion into a career. Now, I’m fully committed to designing engaging and challenging experiences, aiming to create games that are not just fun but leave a lasting impact on players.
